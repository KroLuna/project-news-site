Luna was a stray husky who had been living in the mountains for several months. Doe was exhausted and cold, and he was about to give up hope when he saw Luna. Doe knew that she was trying to help him, so he followed her. Luna led Doe back to the trail, and he was able to find his way back to safety. Doe said that he was so grateful to Luna for saving his life.
